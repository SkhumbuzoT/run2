Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Static sites are delivered within 5-7 business days. Smart sites take 7-10 business days due to the additional features and customization required.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! Smart Site customers receive ongoing monthly maintenance and support for R299/month. Static site customers can purchase support packages as needed.

Can I update my website myself?

Smart Sites include a content management system that allows you to make basic updates. We also provide training and ongoing support to help you manage your site.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

Smart Site customers get monthly updates included. For Static sites, we offer affordable update packages. Major redesigns can be discussed separately.

Do you only work with South African businesses?

Yes, we specialize in serving South African small businesses. This allows us to provide localized solutions, understand the market, and offer support in your timezone.
